Product Description

The Quantum X Pro redefines smartphone excellence with its cutting-edge technology and premium design. The 6.7" AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate delivers buttery-smooth visuals, while the Snapdragon 888 processor ensures lightning-fast performance for gaming and multitasking.

Capture stunning photos with the professional-grade quad camera system featuring a 108MP main sensor, 12MP ultra-wide lens, 8MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, and a dedicated macro camera. The 5000mAh battery with 65W fast charging keeps you powered all day.
